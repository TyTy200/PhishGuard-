About PhishGuard

PhishGuard is an advanced URL security scanner developed as part of the thesis deliverable at the University of Bedfordshire. Our mission is to provide accessible, powerful phishing protection for everyone.

Using state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms and real-time threat intelligence, we help users identify malicious URLs before they cause harm. The system is completely free to use, with no registration required, broswer extension availability, and strict privacy protection.
